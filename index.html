Ingredients

  • 200g cooked sweet potato
  • 150g dark chocolate
  • 60g coconut oil
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 80g almond flour
  • 30g cacao powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Sweet Potato Brownies

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 12-16 slices

Ratings: 4.5/5

This homely and delicious sweet treat is rich and chocolately but best of all - healthy!

Method

Step 1: Prepare

Preheat oven to 180C. Line a square baking tin with parchment paper or grease it lightly.

Step 2: Prepare Sweet Potato

Peel and chop the sweet potato into chunks, then steam or boil until soft. Drain and mash the sweet potato until smooth. You'll need 200g of the mashed sweet potato for the recipe.

Step 3: Melting The Chocolate

In a heatproof bowl, melt the dark chocolate and coconut oil together over simmering water or in short intervals in the microwave.

Step 4: Mix Wet Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the mashed sweet potato, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla extract until well combined. Stir in the melted chocolate mixture until smooth.

Step 5: Add Dry Ingredients

In another bowl, sift together the almond flour, cacao powder, baking powder and salt. Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined. Do not overmix.

Step 6: Bake

Pour the batter into the prepared tin and smooth the top with a spatula. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs attached.

Step 7: Cool and Serve

Allow the brownies to cool completely in the tin before cutting into squares. Serve and enjoy!
